# AngkorGo — Investor Pitch Deck Outline

A ~13-slide deck. Each slide: **the one line it must land**, supporting bullets,
and a visual suggestion. Pairs with `PITCH.md` (one-pager) and `docs/BLURB.md`.
Keep it to one idea per slide; let the app screenshots carry the product.

---

### 1 · Title
- **AngkorGo — Cambodia's everyday super-app.**
- Tagline: *One app. All services. Everywhere in Cambodia.* (EN · ខ្មែរ · 中文)
- 🎨 Logo + Tuki the elephant mascot, clean full-bleed.

### 2 · Problem
- **Line:** *Daily life in Cambodia runs on cash, phone calls, and a dozen disconnected apps.*
- Fragmented services; foreign apps aren't localized (language, payments, trust); no unified, safe, Khmer-first option.
- 🎨 Cluttered "before" collage vs. one clean AngkorGo icon.

### 3 · Solution
- **Line:** *Everything you need, in one trilingual app, paid with KHQR.*
- One login · one wallet · one map · English/ខ្មែរ/中文.
- 🎨 Phone home screen with the service grid.

### 4 · Product — 6 services + safety
- **Line:** *Six businesses, one app.*
- 🛺 Ride · 🍜 Food · 🚗 Rental · 🏠 Stay · 🔧 Repair · 📦 Express — **plus 🚨 Emergency SOS.**
- 🎨 The 6-card grid; SOS highlighted as the differentiator.

### 5 · The wow — Emergency SOS
- **Line:** *A super-app that can also save your life.*
- Membership panic button → nearest police station → live ops dashboard → real-time acknowledgment. (Directs to 117 for immediate danger.)
- 🎨 SOS screen → admin alert → "acknowledged" on the phone (3-frame flow).

### 6 · How it works (demo)
- **Line:** *Request → matched nearby → tracked live → paid in-app.*
- Show one flow end-to-end (Ride or Food), 3–4 screenshots.
- 🎨 Live-tracking map + KHQR payment sheet. (Or a 30-sec screen recording.)

### 7 · Market
- **Line:** *~17M people, mobile-first, and KHQR/Bakong adoption is exploding.*
- Young, urbanizing population; rising smartphone + digital-payment penetration; super-app whitespace.
- 🎨 3 stat callouts (population / smartphone growth / KHQR transactions). *(Insert current figures.)*

### 8 · Business model
- **Line:** *Three revenue lines, provider-friendly economics.*
- **10% commission** per transaction (providers keep 90%) · **$2/mo membership** (SOS + perks) · **B2B** station partner fees.
- 🎨 Simple revenue diagram.

### 9 · Why now / moat
- **Line:** *Deep local fit + a safety edge + an engine that ships verticals in weeks.*
- KHQR-native, trilingual, local trust; one shared backbone (dispatch/tracking/payments) → each vertical is cheap to add; SOS is unique.
- 🎨 "One engine → six services" spoke diagram.

### 10 · Competition
- **Line:** *Global apps aren't built for Cambodia; local players are single-purpose.*
- 2×2 or table: localization (language + KHQR) × breadth (super-app + safety). AngkorGo top-right.
- 🎨 Positioning matrix.

### 11 · Traction / status
- **Line:** *This isn't a concept — the MVP is built and device-verified.*
- All 6 verticals working end-to-end incl. KHQR escrow payments; SOS proven app↔admin; driver app + web admin live; 34 migrations, 5 edge functions.
- 🎨 Checklist of shipped features + "live at angkorgo.app".

### 12 · Roadmap to launch
- **Line:** *Weeks to market, not years.*
- ABA PayWay/Bakong go-live → EAS store submission (customer + driver apps) → police-partner sign-ups → Phnom Penh launch.
- 🎨 Simple 4-step timeline.

### 13 · Team & The Ask
- **Line:** *We've built it. Here's what we need to launch it.*
- Team (founder + key roles/advisors). The raise: $___ for payments go-live, store launch, provider acquisition, first-city GTM.
- Contact: angkorgo.app · [email].
- 🎨 Team photos + the ask in one bold number.

**Delivery tips**
- Open on the SOS story or a 30-sec live demo — lead with the wow.
- One idea per slide; let screenshots do the talking.
- Have the app open on a phone to hand over — it works, so show it.
- Fill the *(bracketed)* market stats and the raise amount before pitching.
